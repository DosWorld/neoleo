10 Examples and Utilites

Examples and utilities are installed into the directory share/neoleo/examples, somewhere on the hard drive. To determine the exact location, type ‘neoleo --version’, and look at the line beginning ‘Datadir:’.

10.1 oleo.pm6

oleo.pm6 is a perl6 script that converts neoleo format from stdin to CSV on stdout. For example: 

./neoleo.pm6 <foo.oleo >foo.csv

BUG: omits cells that have formulae in them.
